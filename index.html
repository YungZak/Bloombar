  1. 01

    Consultation

Our Story

Rooted in Creativity. Designed for Unforgettable Celebrations.

BloomBar began its journey in Los Angeles in 2023 with a simple belief: flowers have the power to transform ordinary moments into unforgettable memories.

The story continued in Charlotte, North Carolina, where BloomBar continued to grow, refining its style and developing a signature approach to modern floral design.

Since 2026, BloomBar has expanded into wedding floral design, offering both full-service wedding florals and thoughtfully crafted everyday arrangements. While weddings have become an important part of our story, our passion for creating beautiful flowers remains at the heart of everything we do.

As BloomBar continues to grow, the next chapter begins in Los Angeles. Starting in 2027, we’re returning to the city where our story first began, bringing our passion for thoughtful floral design to weddings and celebrations across Southern California.

We believe flowers are more than beautiful details — they set the atmosphere, tell a story, and become part of the memories that last long after the celebration is over.

Whether we’re designing an intimate ceremony, creating large-scale floral installations, or crafting a bouquet for a meaningful occasion, every arrangement is created with intention, artistry, and a deep appreciation for life’s most special moments.

Every celebration has a story.
We’re here to help it bloom.
